UCLA Student Demands

The students at the University of California Los Angeles and all over the world are steadfast in our demands for universities to end their support and direct contribution to the Israeli genocide of the Palestinian people and the world-wide war machine. Universities have no place in profiting off the suffering of colonized nations and must immediately cease investments in all Israeli-tied companies to avoid a serious conflict of interest. We reject the UC’s claim that they cannot disclose, divest, and boycott the Israeli entity while at the same time spending an enormous amount of money funding militarized campuses and directly investing in genocide. We will continue our fight until Palestine is fully liberated and our following demands are entirely met without exception:

  1. Divest all UC-wide and UCLA Foundation funds from companies and institutions involved in the Israeli occupation, apartheid, and genocide of the Palestinian people.
  2. Disclose all UC-wide and UCLA Foundation assets and funding sources, including but not limited to contracts, grants, gifts, and investments.
  3. Boycott all academic ties to occupation and genocide. Terminate all gifts, grants, contracts, and endowed positions funded by weapons manufacturers directly perpetuating the genocide of the Palestinians.
  4. Abolish all policing on our campus.
